# WeDigMark Website
Version: 1.0

Status: Active Development

---

# Project Overview

WeDigMark's website is the company's primary digital presence.

Its purpose is to communicate how WeDigMark thinks about marketing, showcase its capabilities through strategic work, educate potential clients, and generate qualified leads.

The website is not intended to be a landing page or a sales funnel. It is a scalable business website that grows alongside the company.

---

# Project Goals

The website should:

- Clearly communicate WeDigMark's positioning.
- Demonstrate strategic thinking before tactical execution.
- Showcase independent strategy projects through the Portfolio.
- Build trust through transparency and educational content.
- Convert qualified visitors into booked strategy calls.

---

# Target Audience

Primary Audience

- Founders
- Ecommerce brands
- DTC businesses
- Businesses looking for long-term marketing partners

Secondary Audience

- Potential hires
- Other marketers
- Agency owners
- Industry peers

---

# Functional Requirements

The website should:

- Be fully responsive.
- Load quickly.
- Be SEO-friendly.
- Support future blog functionality.
- Support future portfolio expansion.
- Be easy to maintain.
- Reuse components wherever possible.

---

# Out of Scope

The website is not intended to:

- Pretend independent projects are client work.
- Use unnecessary animations.
- Become a one-page landing page.
- Sacrifice clarity for visual effects.
- Contain unnecessary sections.

---

# Success Criteria

A visitor should leave believing:

- WeDigMark thinks before it executes.
- The company values strategy over guesswork.
- The work is thoughtful and transparent.
- The company is trustworthy.
- Booking a strategy call is the natural next step.
